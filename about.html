About Me

UI/UX Designer based in Genoa, passionate about human-centred design.

Benedetta Cilento

In 2022 I’ve graduated in modern languages at the university of Genoa but I decided to pursue my passion for design and art and embark on this UI/UX journey to combine my precision with my artistic sense.

My process starts with research. I map user journeys, run interviews, and stress-test assumptions before creating the design. The result: products people actually enjoy using.

When I'm not in Figma, you'll find me making pottery, visiting art exhibitions, or hiking with my dog.
